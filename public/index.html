How Bonsai works
  1. 01
    Read
    Drop any bill. Bonsai reads every line item and flags what doesn't look right.
  2. 02
    Check
    We compare your bill to what others pay, dig through the provider's own terms, and find every discount, loophole, and policy you qualify for.
  3. 03
    Negotiate
    Bonsai autonomously disputes by emailing or calling your provider to get you the lowest price possible.
  4. 04
    Report
    You get a lower bill and a full paper trail. Hundreds saved, zero work.
