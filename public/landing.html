What does it cost?

Nothing during beta. We're operating Bonsai on our own dime so we can learn what real users need before we charge for it.

What kinds of bills does Bonsai handle?

Anywhere money quietly leaks. Insurance premiums, utility bills, internet and phone plans, recurring subscriptions, lines of credit, medical bills, anything with a contract or a rate that drifts up year over year. Bonsai reads the bill, the contract, and any supporting docs you give it, then disputes whatever doesn't add up.

Is my data safe?

Bills, transcripts, and account data live under your account only. We don't sell, share, or train external models on your data. Read the Privacy Policy for the full rundown of what we store and why.

Can the AI agent call and email?

Yes — both. Bonsai drafts appeals, sends emails to billing departments, and dials phone numbers and talks to real reps. Every outbound message goes out only after your one-click approval, and you're CC'd on every email.

How does it pick email vs. phone?

Bonsai picks the channel based on the dispute. Some appeals only work in writing — formal disputes, paper-trail required. Others only work on a call where you can apply real-time pressure or navigate a phone tree. You can override Bonsai's pick in Settings.

What if it can't lower my bill?

The agent escalates: another round of negotiation, a re-aimed appeal at a different department, or a hand-off back to you with everything documented so you (or an advocate) can take it from there. No save, no charge — Bonsai is free during beta either way.

What if Bonsai gets something wrong?

You see every finding before any letter goes out, and every email and call before it lands. If something's off, you reject the draft and Bonsai redrafts. The point is to give you leverage, not surprises.
