What Bonsai does

An agent on your side of the table.

01

Grounded audit

Every finding cites the exact line it came from. No hallucinated charges, no vague gripes — just the rows that don't match what you were promised.

02

Appeal in your voice

A specific, signed-off-by-you letter or email — not a template. Cites the contract, the policy, and the math in the tone you set in Settings.

03

Email negotiation

Bonsai writes to the billing office, reads the reply, and writes back — on a real domain you can verify. You're BCC'd on every message.

04

Voice negotiation

For the bills that need a phone call, Bonsai dials, navigates the menu, talks to a real rep, and saves the transcript to your dashboard.

How it works

Four steps. You stay in the loop the whole way.

  1. 01

    Upload a bill.

    PDFs, photos, or screenshots. Anything recurring works — insurance, utilities, internet, medical, subscriptions. Got the contract or claim doc that goes with it? Upload that too. It sharpens the audit.

  2. 02

    Bonsai finds the opportunities.

    Duplicate charges, prices that drifted off-contract, fees that shouldn't be there, rate hikes you never agreed to, promos that quietly expired, plans that no longer match the market. Every finding cites the exact line on the bill.

  3. 03

    Negotiates by email or phone.

    Bonsai drafts the appeal in your voice, picks the channel that fits — a written dispute where one's required, a real phone call where it isn't — and works the case. Every offer comes back to you for a one-click approval.

  4. 04

    You wake up paying less.

    Bonsai works 24/7 — running disputes, chasing stalled threads, and surfacing the win the moment a vendor lowers your bill. Put your agent on a cadence and it renegotiates on its own from there.

FAQ

The questions everyone asks first.

What does it cost?

Nothing during preview. We're operating Bonsai on our own dime so we can learn what real users need before we charge for it.

What kinds of bills does Bonsai handle?

Anywhere money quietly leaks. Insurance premiums, utility bills, internet and phone plans, recurring subscriptions, lines of credit, medical bills, anything with a contract or a rate that drifts up year over year. Bonsai reads the bill, the contract, and any supporting docs you give it, then disputes whatever doesn't add up.

Is my data safe?

Bills, transcripts, and account data live under your account only. We don't sell, share, or train external models on your data. Read the Privacy Policy for the full rundown of what we store and why.

Email or phone — who picks?

Bonsai picks the channel based on the dispute (some appeals only work in writing; some only work on a call), but you can override it in Settings. Either way, you approve the outbound message before it goes.

What if the rep refuses?

The agent escalates: another round of negotiation, a re-aimed appeal at a different department, or a hand-off back to you with everything documented so a human (you or an advocate) can take it from there.

What if Bonsai gets something wrong?

You see every finding before any letter goes out, and every email and call before it lands. If something's off, you reject the draft and Bonsai redrafts. The point is to give you leverage, not surprises.
